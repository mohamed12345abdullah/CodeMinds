Our Team

Web Design Committee
The Web Design Committee focuses on creating and maintaining websites that are user-friendly, visually appealing, and accessible.

OC Committee
The OC Committee ensures smooth execution of events by coordinating logistics, managing schedules, and overseeing event operations to ensure everything runs efficiently and effectively.

Media Committee
The Media Committee is responsible for capturing and sharing the essence of our events through photography, videography, and social media, ensuring a strong and positive online presence.

Marketing Committee
The Marketing Committee drives engagement and awareness by developing creative campaigns, managing promotional activities, and leveraging various marketing channels to reach our target audience.

HR Committee
The HR Committee focuses on recruitment, training, and development, ensuring that we have a motivated and well-prepared team to achieve our goals and maintain a positive organizational culture.

PR Committee
The PR Committee manages the organization's public image by building relationships with media, crafting press releases, and handling communications to maintain a positive and professional reputation.
